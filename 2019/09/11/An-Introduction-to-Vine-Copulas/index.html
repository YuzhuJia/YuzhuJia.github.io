3. 刻画相关性

  • C is unique.
  • Joint density: $f\left(x_{1}, x_{2}\right)=c_{12}\left(F_{1}\left(x_{1}\right), F_{2}\left(x_{2}\right)\right) \cdot f_{1}\left(x_{1}\right) \cdot f_{2}\left(x_{2}\right)$
  • Then we’ll have conditional density: $f\left(x_{2} | x_{1}\right)=c_{12}\left(F_{1}\left(x_{1}\right), F_{2}\left(x_{2}\right)\right) \cdot f_{2}\left(x_{2}\right)$

n Dimensions

Proof:
$$
f\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)=\prod_{t=2}^{n} f\left(x_{t} | x_{1}, \cdots, x_{t-1}\right) f\left(x_{1}\right)
$$

5. Vine Copula

RV Copula(Regula)
  • 树结构
    • Tree j has $n + 1 - j$ nodes and $n - j$ edges;
    • 边变成下一层节点；
    • 上一层两条边共用节点 = 这一层作为节点相连。
  • Regular vine distribution
    • Each edge corresponds to a pair-copula density；
    • The density of a regular vine distribution = $\prod$ pair copula densities * $\prod$ the marginal densities.
  • DV, CV $\in$ R.
DV Copula

每一行都是一棵树。

CV Copula (Canonical 正则；典范)
