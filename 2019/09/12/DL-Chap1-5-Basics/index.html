2. Basics(partial)
Linear Algebra

  • Decomposition

    • Eigendecomposition

      对称矩阵的谱分解 $A=V \operatorname{diag}(\lambda) V^{-1}$；(涉及复数)

      实对称 $A=Q \Lambda Q^{T}$. (特征值，特征向量 实)

    • SVD

      非对称矩阵的“谱分解”——奇异值分解 $A=U D V^{T}$；

      由$A^H A$的谱分解得来；

      $A^{+}=V D^{+} U^{T}$.

Probability and Information Theory

  • Another viewpoint of Shannon Entropy: The minimum expected binary coding length for a random variable signal from P and coding as P.

  • Minimize Cross Entropy is equivalent to maxium likelihood in many cases.

    Proof？

  • Common Functions

    • Logistic sigmoid: $\sigma(X)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-X}}$

    • Softplus: $\zeta(x)=\log \left(1+e^{x}\right)$

      是Logistic sigmoid的积分。

    • Relu: $f(x)=\max (0, x)$

  • Consistency

    • Week Consistency: convergence in probability of $\hat{\theta}_n$ to $\theta$.

    • Strong Consistency: almost sure convergence of $\hat{\theta}_n$ to $\theta$.

    • Asymptotic unbiasedness is not equivalent to consistency.

      Consistency $\to$ Asymptotic unbiasedness?

      For example, the estimator $\frac{1}{N-1} \sum_{i} x_{i}$ is a consistent estimator for the sample mean, but it’s not unbiased.

      Asymptotic unbiasedness $\to$ Consistency?

      an unbiased estimator $\hat{\theta}=x^{(1)}$ with $E\left(\hat{\theta}\right)=\theta$. But it is not the case that $\hat{\theta} \rightarrow \theta$ as $m \rightarrow \infty$.

Numerical Computation

  • Constrained Optimization — KKT condition

    • Necessary for any optimization problem when strong duality
      holds;

    • Sufficient for convex optimization problems (And can
      induce that the strong duality holds);

    • Note: There exist convex optimization problems with the
      strong duality failed;

    • KKT condition

      最优点梯度为0：$\nabla f_{0}\left(x^{\star}\right)+\sum_{i=1}^{m} \lambda_{i}^{\star} \nabla f_{i}\left(x^{\star}\right)+\sum_{i=1}^{p} \nu_{i}^{\star} \nabla h_{i}\left(x^{\star}\right)=0$

      原始可行：

      $\begin{aligned} f_{i}\left(x^{\star}\right) & \leq 0, \quad i=1, \ldots, m \ h_{i}\left(x^{\star}\right) &=0, \quad i=1, \ldots, p \end{aligned}$

      对偶可行：$\lambda_{i}^{\star} \geq 0, \quad i=1, \dots, m$

      互补松弛条件：$\lambda_{i}^{\star} f_{i}\left(x^{\star}\right)=0, \quad i=1, \ldots, m$

Machine Learning Basics

  • Capacity

    • a model’s capacity is its ability to fit a wide variety of functions；

    • Models with low capacity may struggle to fit the training set；

    • Models with high capacity can overfit.

    • 直观来看网络的capacity与网络参数量成正比。

      考虑到参数间互相coupling、correlating的现象（某些参数能力很强，总能做出正确的事，从而网络学会了偷懒，其他某些参数（units）只是在机械地模仿这些“superstar“unit的输出结果），也即网络capacity的折损。

      需要一些手段来de-coupling、de-correlating，让每个units都主动去学，各司其职，分担工作。这些手段就是regularizations。

  • Control the capacity

    • Hypothesis space (限定)

    • Use Non-parametric models (increase capacity)

    • Regularization

      Dropout：让部分神经元不参加本次训练，激励偷懒的神经元训练。

  • Generalization

    • The ability to perform well on previously unobserved inputs；
    • Generalization Error: The expected value of the error on a new input.
    • 一个网络的generalization能力是由网络的capacity和训练数据量共同决定的。

  • Relations

    • regularization能够帮助兑现网络的capacity，更强的capacity配合更大的training data会得到更强的generalization capacity of the model；

    • When the model: optimal capacity + a large gap between training and generalization error.

      In this situation, we may be able to reduce this gap by gathering more training examples.

  • Hyperparameters
    • In most cases, the values of hyperparameters are not adapted by the learning algorithm;
    • e.g. The degree of the polynomial in polynomial regression
      The λ value used to control the strength of weight decay
    • Validation Sets: used to “train”the hyperparameters.
