  • Consistency

    • Week Consistency: convergence in probability of $\hat{\theta}_n$ to $\theta$.

    • Strong Consistency: almost sure convergence of $\hat{\theta}_n$ to $\theta$.

    • Asymptotic unbiasedness is not equivalent to consistency.

      Consistency $\to$ Asymptotic unbiasedness?

      For example, the estimator $\frac{1}{N-1} \sum_{i} x_{i}$ is a consistent estimator for the sample mean, but it‚Äôs not unbiased.

      Asymptotic unbiasedness $\to$ Consistency?

      an unbiased estimator $\hat{\theta}=x^{(1)}$ with $E\left(\hat{\theta}\right)=\theta$. But it is not the case that $\hat{\theta} \rightarrow \theta$ as $m \rightarrow \infty$.
